About Me

By Adrian Lopez

Summary

I was born and raised in Anaheim, CA and moved to Utah back in 2018.
California was too crowded and way too expensive so I felt moving to
Utah was the right move. Although Utah's weather is completely different
than California's weather, I definitely prefer Utah over California. 
Aside from the weather and the amount of people California has, jobs out 
here are a lot better as well. I am currently an Onsite Manager for a Staffing 
Agency that I've been with since I moved out here. I've been in the staffing 
industry for 4 years ao I figured staying in the industry was the smart decision. 
However, as much as I enjoy giving people jobs, I felt I needed to start doing 
something I actually enjoy doing like coding. With that being said, listed below 
are things I enjoy doing as well aside from coding.

Gaming

I enjoy playing on the PC as well as on the phone.
Action games are usually what I prefer playing.

Reading

I enjoy reading, however, I prefer reading hard copy books,
maingly because using a <EPUB> type books distract me.

Hiking
